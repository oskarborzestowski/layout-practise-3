Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choise of cheese, vegetables, or meals.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingriedients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butte or oil
  • optionally filings cheese diced vegetables cooked meals or herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk a flutter texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil
  3. Cook the omlette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coot the surface
  4. Add fillings:When the eggs begin to set at the edged but are still sligtly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chasen fillings over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute then slide it anto a plate
  6. Enjoy:Serve hat, with additionaly salt and pepper if needed
